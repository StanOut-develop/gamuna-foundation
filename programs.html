Programs & Events

What we do, where we've been, and what's coming next.

Our Core Programs

01

Awareness Programs

Community outreach sessions covering health and hygiene, education, employment pathways, law and order, and "gutpela sidaun" (good living). We bring information directly to urban and rural communities across Madang Province.

02

Workshops & Training

Hands-on skill-building sessions in leadership, entrepreneurship, and personal development — facilitated by our Training Committee and subject-matter experts.

03

Mentorship & Confidence Building

One-on-one and group mentorship programs that foster self-worth among young people, with community involvement at the core of every interaction.

04

Motivational Events & Seminars

Inspiring gatherings where youth share success stories, hear from role models, and build a supportive peer network that replaces negative influences with positive direction.

05

Community Service Projects

Youth-led initiatives that promote social responsibility and environmental stewardship, partnering with local businesses to create internships and job pathways.

06

Donation Drives

Coordinated drives to collect and distribute resources to vulnerable community members, building a culture of generosity and mutual support.
